Introduction

Thirdeye – Emotion Tracker (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Thirdeye – Emotion Tracker (“the App”). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information when you use the App:

  • Name & Email Address – when you sign in using Google Sign-In or Email/Password.
  • Profile Information – basic details provided during authentication.

1.2 Usage & Quiz Data

To help track emotional wellness, we collect your quiz responses, emotion tracking history, and app usage patterns (if analytics are enabled).

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect device information (model, OS version), log data (crash reports, performance analytics), and Firebase-generated identifiers. This information helps us improve app performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and operate the emotion tracking features.
  • Store your quiz results and wellness history.
  • Authenticate you securely.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. Data Storage & Security

We use secure, industry-standard services for data handling:

  • Firebase Services – Google Sign-In, Authentication, Analytics (if enabled).
  • Supabase – Stores quiz responses and emotional wellness data and other user-specific app data.

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service Providers (Firebase, Google APIs, Supabase) to operate the App.
  • Legal Requirements if required by law, regulation, or government request.

Your data is never shared for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, update or correct your information, request deletion of your account and related data, and stop using the App at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

6. Children’s Privacy

Thirdeye – Emotion Tracker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active or as required to provide the App’s services. If you delete your account, all associated personal data and quiz records will be removed from our servers.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that have their own Privacy Policies: Google Firebase, Google Sign-In, and Supabase. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for additional information.
