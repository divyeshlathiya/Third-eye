Thirdeye — Account & Data Deletion Request

Use this page to request permanent deletion of your account and associated data from the Thirdeye – Emotion Tracker app.

To delete your account, please complete the form below. Submitting will open your email client with a pre-filled message to our support team at developer.thirdeyeemotion@gmail.com. If you prefer, you may send an email directly to that address.

Processing time: Once we receive your request we will verify ownership and delete your data within 48–72 hours. We may contact the registered email if additional verification is needed.

If you do not receive confirmation after 72 hours, email us at developer.thirdeyeemotion@gmail.com.
